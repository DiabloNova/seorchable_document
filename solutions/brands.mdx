If your brand isn't appearing in AI-generated answers, you're invisible to a fast-growing share of your audience. جستجوپذیر gives brand marketers a single workspace to track where they appear — and where they don't — across ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity, and to take targeted action on the content that will earn more citations.

## The Challenge for Brands

AI search engines are increasingly answering questions instead of returning a list of blue links. When someone asks ChatGPT "what's the best tool for X?", the model synthesises a response from its training data and web retrieval — and it mentions only the brands it considers authoritative and well-documented. Brands that appear in those answers win organic reach, credibility, and high-intent traffic without paying for ads. Brands that don't appear are simply absent from the conversation.

## Key Use Cases

**Track branded keyword citations.** Find out whether your brand is being mentioned when people ask AI engines about your product category. جستجوپذیر surfaces every prompt variation where a competitor is named but you aren't.

**Identify content gaps.** Run a competitive citation audit to discover the exact topics and questions where rivals appear in AI answers but your content doesn't. Each gap becomes an actionable content brief.

**Optimise high-intent pages for AI citation.** Use AI-readiness scores to prioritise which existing pages will yield the highest citation lift per hour of editing effort. The platform tells you precisely what to change — structured data, depth of coverage, authoritative sourcing.

**Report AI visibility metrics to stakeholders.** Export shareable reports that translate technical citation data into business-friendly metrics: estimated impressions from AI answers, share-of-voice trend, and projected revenue impact of reaching the next citation tier.

## Metrics Brands Track

| Metric | What it measures |
|---|---|
| **Citation rate** | Percentage of tracked queries where your brand is cited in the AI answer |
| **Share of AI voice** | Your citations as a proportion of all citations across your competitive set |
| **AI-readiness score** | Page-level score (0–100) predicting how likely a page is to be cited by AI engines |
| **Competitor citation gap** | Number of queries where a specific competitor is cited and you are not |
