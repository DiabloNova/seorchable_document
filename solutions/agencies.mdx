## How Agencies Use جستجوپذیر

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your agency account">
    Sign up for an agency plan or contact sales to migrate an existing account. Your agency account becomes the parent workspace from which all client workspaces are created and managed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add client workspaces">
    Create a dedicated workspace for each client. Workspaces are fully isolated — each client's data, keyword tracking, and reports are never visible to other clients, ensuring confidentiality across your portfolio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure per-client tracking">
    Within each workspace, set the client's primary domain, target keywords, and up to five competitor domains. Use your saved onboarding template to populate standard settings instantly, then fine-tune for the client's specific niche.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run bulk AI visibility audits">
    Once client workspaces are configured, trigger a portfolio-level audit from your agency dashboard. جستجوپذیر scans all client domains simultaneously and populates baseline citation rate, AI-readiness scores, and competitive gap data for every account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate and share client reports">
    Select any combination of client workspaces and click **Generate Reports**. Choose white-label output, pick your date range, and download a PDF or send a shareable link directly to the client — all without leaving the agency dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use the **Prompt Library** to build a standardised set of AI query templates for each client vertical (e-commerce, SaaS, professional services, and so on). Consistent prompts across clients make competitive benchmarking meaningful and give your team a repeatable research process that scales without additional headcount.
</Tip>
