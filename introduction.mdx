## What is AI Search Optimization?

AI Search Optimization (AEO) is the practice of making your content more visible and more accurately cited by AI-powered search and answer engines. Unlike classical search engines that return a list of blue links, LLM-based engines synthesize a direct answer and cite only a handful of sources. Getting cited — and cited accurately — requires a different set of signals than traditional SEO.

جستجوپذیر tracks those signals continuously across more than 20 AI engines, surfaces the gaps between where you appear and where you should appear, and gives you specific guidance to close that gap.
