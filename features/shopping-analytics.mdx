AI search engines have entered the shopping experience. When a user asks ChatGPT "what's the best ergonomic office chair under $500?" or prompts Perplexity to compare noise-cancelling headphones, the model doesn't just return links — it recommends specific products by name, cites specific pages, and often explains why one product is better than another. For e-commerce brands and product marketers, this shift creates both a significant opportunity and a new competitive battleground that traditional tools don't measure. جستجوپذیر Shopping Analytics is built specifically to track, analyze, and improve your products' position in AI-driven shopping recommendations.

## How AI Shopping Works

Shopping-intent queries — "best X," "compare X and Y," "what should I buy for Z" — are among the most common and commercially valuable prompts submitted to AI engines. When a model processes a shopping query, it draws on product page content, structured data, review signals, and pricing information to construct a recommendation. The products that appear, and their order, depend heavily on how well their pages communicate key attributes to the AI model.

Unlike a traditional PLA (Product Listing Ad) or organic product ranking, AI shopping recommendations are generated fresh for each query. There are no fixed positions — your product either earns a mention in the response or it doesn't. Shopping Analytics monitors this space continuously so you always know where your products stand.

## Set Up Product Tracking

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Shopping Analytics">
    Open **Shopping Analytics** from your جستجوپذیر dashboard. If this is your first time, you'll be prompted to set up your product catalog before the dashboard populates with data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your Product Catalog or Import from Your Store URL">
    Enter your store URL and جستجوپذیر will crawl your product pages automatically, importing titles, descriptions, prices, and URLs into your product inventory. Alternatively, upload a product CSV for precise control over which products are tracked. You can also add individual product URLs manually for selective tracking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Tracked Shopping Prompts">
    In the **Shopping Prompts** tab, add the queries you want to monitor — for example, "best wireless headphones for gym," "affordable standing desk under $300," or "compare [your brand] vs [competitor brand]." جستجوپذیر will submit these prompts to tracked AI models on a regular cadence and log which products are recommended in each response.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor Recommendation Positions">
    Return to the Shopping Analytics dashboard to view recommendation position data for each tracked product and prompt. Use the **Position Trend** chart to track whether products are moving up or down in AI recommendations over time. Filter by AI model to see if performance varies between ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The single highest-impact improvement most e-commerce pages can make is ensuring AI models can instantly find four pieces of information: a clear product name, a specific price, a concise benefits-led description (not a spec list), and a valid `Product` schema block. AI models synthesizing shopping recommendations heavily favor pages that surface these signals without ambiguity. Audit your product pages against these four criteria before pursuing any other optimization.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Shopping Analytics works best as part of a complete AI search strategy. Use it alongside [Content Management](/features/content-management) to ensure your product pages score well on content depth and structure — not just pricing and availability. Products with strong on-page content consistently earn more AI recommendations than products with sparse descriptions, even when the prices are comparable.
</Note>
