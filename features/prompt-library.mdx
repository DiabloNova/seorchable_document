In traditional SEO, you optimize for keywords. In AI search, you optimize for prompts — the natural-language questions and instructions users feed into ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity before those models decide which sources to cite. جستجوپذیر's Prompt Library gives you a centralized, searchable database of the prompts that are already triggering AI responses in your space, complete with data on which prompts cite your pages, which cite competitors, and which represent untapped opportunities your content doesn't yet cover.

## What Is the Prompt Library?

The Prompt Library is a continuously updated collection of real user prompts that have triggered AI-generated responses in the topics and keywords you track. Think of it as a keyword research tool built specifically for the AI search era — instead of showing you search volume for individual terms, it shows you the full conversational queries people are actually submitting to AI engines, along with citation outcomes for each prompt.

Every prompt in the library is enriched with:
- **The AI model(s)** that processed it
- **Citation results** — which pages were cited, in what order
- **Your site's citation status** — whether you were cited, a competitor was cited, or no source was cited at all
- **Prompt category** — automatically classified by topic, intent, and funnel stage

## Build a Prompt Research Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Discovered Prompts in the Library">
    Open **Prompt Library** from the dashboard sidebar. The default view shows all discovered prompts sorted by citation frequency — the prompts that are driving the most AI responses in your tracked topics. Scan this list to orient yourself to what users in your space are actually asking AI engines.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by AI Model or Keyword">
    Use the **Model** filter to narrow results to prompts from ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity specifically — different models attract different query patterns. Use the **Keyword** filter to find prompts containing terms relevant to a specific product, topic, or campaign. Combine filters to zero in on exactly the prompt landscape you're researching.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Prompts to a Collection">
    Select the prompts most relevant to your current focus and click **Add to Collection**. Create a new collection or add to an existing one. Collections keep your research organized by campaign or content initiative and make it easy to share focused prompt sets with your content team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use Prompts to Guide Content Creation">
    Export a collection or open it in the **Content Brief** view. جستجوپذیر maps each prompt to the content gaps it exposes on your site, giving your writers a clear brief: here are the questions being asked, here's who is currently being cited, and here's the type of content that would earn citations for these prompts. Feed this directly into your editorial calendar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Competitor prompts — where a rival is being cited instead of you — are your single best source of high-confidence content ideas. These prompts have already proven demand (real users are asking them), AI models are already forming an answer (so the citation opportunity is real), and your competitor's existence as a citation proves the opportunity is winnable. Filter the library by **Citation Status: Competitor** to pull this list and prioritize it above all other content gap research.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do prompts come from?">
    Prompts in the library are sourced through جستجوپذیر's ongoing monitoring of AI search activity across the topics and keywords you configure in your account. The platform submits representative queries to tracked AI models, collects citation data from the responses, and extracts the prompt patterns driving those results. No individual user data is collected — prompts represent aggregate query patterns, not personal conversations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I add custom prompts to track?">
    Go to **Prompt Library → Custom Prompts** and click **Add Prompt**. Enter the prompt text and select which AI models to test it against. جستجوپذیر will submit the prompt to each selected model on a regular monitoring cadence and add the citation results to your library. Custom prompts are ideal for tracking very specific product questions, brand mentions, or competitive comparisons that automated discovery might not surface.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
