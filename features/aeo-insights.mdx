AEO Insights is جستجوپذیر's answer to a fundamental shift in search: AI engines like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity no longer just rank links — they cite specific web pages as sources within their generated responses. Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) Insights gives you full visibility into those citation events, so you always know when your brand appears, which pages are driving citations, and how you stack up against competitors.

## What Is an AI Citation Event?

When a user asks ChatGPT "what's the best project management tool for remote teams?" and the model responds by referencing your blog post or product page, that is an AI citation event. Unlike a traditional click-through from a search results page, your content is woven directly into the AI's answer — making citation placement a critical metric for brand authority in the AI search era.

AEO Insights automatically detects and logs these events across all major AI search engines, giving you a unified view of your citation footprint.

## How to Use AEO Insights

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to AEO Insights">
    From your جستجوپذیر dashboard, select **AEO Insights** in the left sidebar under the **Analytics** section. You'll land on the citation overview screen showing your total citation events for the current period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the Date Range">
    Use the date picker in the top-right corner to select a custom range or choose a preset window (last 7 days, last 30 days, last 90 days). Narrowing the range helps you correlate citation changes with specific content updates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by AI Model or Keyword">
    Apply the **Model** filter to isolate citations from a specific AI engine, or use the **Keyword** filter to see which search queries triggered citations for your content. Combine both filters for granular analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export the Report">
    Click **Export** to download your citation data as a CSV or PDF. Use the exported report for internal presentations, client reporting, or feeding data into your own analytics stack.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What counts as an AI citation event?">
    An AI citation event is recorded whenever a monitored AI engine includes one of your tracked URLs as a source in a generated response. This covers both explicit hyperlink citations and instances where the AI references your brand or page content without a direct link, depending on how the model surfaces sources.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How often is data updated?">
    Citation data is refreshed continuously and typically surfaces in your dashboard within minutes of detection. For high-frequency queries, you may see near-real-time updates. Trend charts aggregate data hourly to keep visualizations readable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I track competitor citations?">
    Yes. Add competitor domains in **Settings → Competitor Tracking** and AEO Insights will monitor their citation events alongside yours. You can view competitor citation volume, source pages, and model breakdowns in the same filtered views you use for your own data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Use AEO Insights together with [LLM Analytics](/features/llm-analytics) for the full picture. AEO Insights tells you *when* and *where* citations happen; LLM Analytics explains *why* — showing which prompts, keywords, and content patterns drive AI models to prefer your pages over competitors.
</Tip>
