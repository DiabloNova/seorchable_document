This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a brand-new جستجوپذیر account to a live AI visibility report. The entire process takes under five minutes, requires no credit card, and does not involve any code changes on your site. By the end, you'll have real data showing exactly where your pages appear — and where they don't — across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and more.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Navigate to [app.seorchable.com/signup](https://app.seorchable.com/signup) and register with your work email address or Google account.

    - Your free trial activates immediately upon sign-up.
    - No credit card is required for the trial period.
    - You can invite team members to your workspace at any time after account creation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your website">
    From your dashboard, click **Add Website** and enter your root domain (e.g., `example.com`).

    Next, verify ownership using one of the two available methods:

    - **DNS TXT record** — Add the provided TXT record to your domain's DNS settings. Propagation typically takes 1–10 minutes.
    - **Meta tag** — Paste the provided `<meta>` tag into the `<head>` of your homepage and click **Verify Now**.

    Once verification succeeds, جستجوپذیر begins crawling your site and indexing your pages for AI visibility tracking.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure tracked keywords and competitors">
    Define the scope of your AI visibility monitoring:

    1. Enter the **keywords and topics** you want to track. These are the queries you'd like your brand to appear in when users ask AI engines for recommendations or answers.
    2. Add up to five **competitor domains** to benchmark your visibility against theirs.
    3. Select the **AI engines** to monitor — ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and others are enabled by default.

    جستجوپذیر immediately begins querying the selected AI engines with your tracked keywords and logs the results.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View your first AI visibility report">
    Head to the **AEO Insights** tab in your dashboard. Within minutes of completing setup, you'll see:

    - Which of your pages are being cited by AI engines
    - The frequency and context of those citations
    - A citation-share breakdown across the AI engines you've enabled
    - Initial recommendations for pages that are close to appearing but not yet cited

    Your data refreshes continuously, so scores and rankings update in real time as AI engines respond.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up alerts for ranking changes">
    Keep track of meaningful changes without checking the dashboard manually:

    1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**.
    2. Enable **Ranking Change Alerts** and set your preferred threshold (e.g., notify me when citation frequency drops or rises by more than 10%).
    3. Choose your delivery channel: email, Slack, or webhook.

    Alerts fire as soon as جستجوپذیر detects a change that meets your threshold, so you can act quickly when your visibility shifts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Start by tracking your top 10 highest-traffic pages rather than your entire site. These pages already have audience demand and are the most likely candidates for AI citations — getting them optimized first delivers the fastest visibility gains.
</Tip>
